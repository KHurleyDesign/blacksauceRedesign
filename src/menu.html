Sample Menu

The events we cater vary in size from a couple dozen to a couple hundred people. They vary in theme from informal family celebration to formal seated dinner. We don’t operate off of stock menus because most of the menus we generate are seasonal, specific to the context of event, and developed in close consultation with our clients. Above are some examples of menus we’ve done in recent months, but again, they are event specific. The best way – in fact, the only way – to know whether or not we are suited to do an event is to contact us. We would love to hear from you.
